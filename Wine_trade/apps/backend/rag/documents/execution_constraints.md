# Execution Constraints and Limits

## Capital Constraints

### Portfolio Limits
- Maximum trade value: 10% of total portfolio value
- Minimum trade value: $100
- Reserve capital: Maintain 20% cash reserve

### Daily Limits
- Maximum trades per day: 5
- Maximum total value per day: 20% of portfolio
- Cooldown period: 1 hour between trades

## Timing Constraints

### Market Hours
- Trading allowed: 24/7 (simulated market)
- Best execution times: Market open hours
- Avoid trading during low liquidity periods

### Holding Periods
- Minimum holding period: 1 day
- Maximum holding period: 2 years
- Review positions weekly

## Quantity Constraints

### Per-Trade Limits
- Minimum quantity: 1 bottle
- Maximum quantity: 10 bottles per trade
- Total position limit: 20 bottles per asset

### Portfolio Limits
- Maximum total bottles: 100
- Minimum diversification: 3 different assets
- Maximum concentration: 20% per asset

## Risk Constraints

### Risk Limits
- Maximum risk score: 0.7
- Stop-loss: 15% loss threshold
- Maximum drawdown: 25% of portfolio

### Confidence Requirements
- Minimum confidence for BUY: 0.6
- Minimum confidence for SELL: 0.6
- Minimum confidence for arbitrage: 0.7

## Regional Constraints

### Geographic Limits
- Verify shipping availability
- Check import/export restrictions
- Consider tax implications
- Validate regional regulations

### Cross-Region Trading
- Maximum 3 regions in portfolio
- Regional concentration limit: 40%
- Verify logistics feasibility

## Compliance Constraints

### Regulatory Requirements
- All trades must pass compliance checks
- Document all decisions
- Maintain audit trail
- Follow local regulations

### Documentation
- Required documents must be available
- Maintain records for 7 years
- Provide explanations for all decisions

## System Constraints

### Technical Limits
- Maximum concurrent simulations: 10
- Maximum pending approvals: 5
- API rate limits: 100 requests/minute

### Data Constraints
- Minimum data points for analysis: 10
- Maximum lookback period: 2 years
- Data freshness: Within 24 hours
