# Trading Strategy Documentation

## Core Strategies

### 1. Arbitrage Strategy
- Identify price differences between regions
- Buy in lower-priced region, sell in higher-priced region
- Minimum profit margin: 5% after transaction costs
- Requires confidence >= 0.7

### 2. Value Investing Strategy
- Focus on undervalued assets with strong fundamentals
- Long-term holding period (6+ months)
- Target ROI: 10-20% annually
- Lower confidence threshold acceptable (>= 0.6)

### 3. Momentum Trading Strategy
- Follow price trends and market momentum
- Short-term holding (1-3 months)
- Higher confidence required (>= 0.75)
- Quick entry and exit based on trend changes

## Strategy Selection Criteria

### Asset Characteristics
- Vintage quality and reputation
- Producer reputation
- Region market dynamics
- Historical price performance

### Market Conditions
- Current market pulse by region
- Arbitrage opportunity availability
- Liquidity conditions
- Volatility levels

### Portfolio Fit
- Alignment with existing holdings
- Diversification benefits
- Risk profile compatibility
- Capital allocation constraints

## Execution Guidelines

### Entry Criteria
- Clear signal from analysis
- Meets confidence threshold
- Passes compliance checks
- Within risk limits

### Exit Criteria
- Target ROI achieved
- Stop-loss triggered
- Better opportunity identified
- Strategy change required

### Position Management
- Regular review of open positions
- Adjust based on market changes
- Rebalance portfolio as needed
- Monitor for exit signals

## Performance Metrics

### Strategy Performance Tracking
- ROI by strategy type
- Win rate (successful trades)
- Average holding period
- Risk-adjusted returns

### Optimization
- Review strategy performance quarterly
- Adjust parameters based on results
- Test new strategies in simulation
- Learn from outcomes
